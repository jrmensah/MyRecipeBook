Egg Sauce

Nigeria

Ingredients

2 large tomatoes

1 large onion

1 scotch bonnet pepper

2 tablespoons of cooking oil (any type)

4 or 5 eggs (depending on number of people eating

Seasonings (suya pepper, garlic powder, thyme, etc)

Directions

Dice onion, tomatoes and 1 scotch bonnet pepper(depending on how hot you want it to be) or can be replaced with black pepper.

Add the diced onions, tomatoes and pepper into a pot, and cook until some of the juices are dried up.

Then add a tablespoon or 2 (enough to cook the eggs) to the mixture

Fry for a little bit, then add seasonings (suya pepper gotten from Tesco,any other type of seasoning pepper can be used, garlic powder, Nigerian knorr seasoning cubes stock can be used instead but not diluted with water, thyme, pinch of salt and any other seasoning you like)

Then add eggs and cook until well done
